President

Represent student senate with other institutions and deal with faculty, staff and administration. President will assign the leader of committees. He/she will also need to serve as Chairman of the executive board.

Vice President I

Assist the President in carrying out duties. Vp II will provide the other point of view towards all the student Committees.

Vice President II

Sit on the budget panel and serve as a chairperson of Inter Club Council. The duty also including maintaining the relationship with the clubs.

Treasurer

The person who’s in charge of all the budgets. He/ she will maintain accurate record of expense of the Student Senate. This position is responsible for knowing the details of money and its effect on the Student Senate.

Secretary

This position will be recording the minutes of the meetings with details like votes, topics been discussed, et cetera. Secretary also needs to prepare and distribute meeting agendas.

About Student Senate Voters' Guide

Student Senate Voters' Guide is a tool to help students know more about Rockville Campus student senate before deciding whom to vote for. The quiz in the tool will help voters to know which candidates have more similar thoughts towards student life in Montgomery College Rockville campus.

What does student senate do?

Student Senate is a group of students that helps you to improve student life experience in all different kinds of aspects. They host events, advocate for policy changes, have influence on clubs and represent Montgomery College Rockville Campus in public arena. Join the student senate today and become the voice of students!

Do you know?

In Student senate, there are 7 committees to help you improve your school experience. There are student issue, food, policy and archives, campus activity, budget panel, public outreach and fundraising! Contact them if you have any suggestions or questions for them.
