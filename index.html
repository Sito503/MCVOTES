What does student senate do?

Student Senate is a group of students that helps you to improve student life experience in all different kinds of aspects. They host events, advocate for policy changes, have influence on clubs and represent Montgomery College Rockville Campus in public arena. Join the student senate today and become the voice of students!

Do you know?

In Student senate, there are 7 committees to help you improve your school experience. There are student issue, food, policy and archives, campus activity, budget panel, public outreach and fundraising! Contact them if you have any suggestions or questions for them.
